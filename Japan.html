WELCOME TO JAPAN

Places you should visit

Tokyo, the heart and center of modern Japanese culture and technology, so many things you can do you will never be bored

Mt Fuji, the tallest mountain known for its stunning scenery and is a popular tourist spot not only for photos but hiking, camping and relaxation

Himeji and cherry blossoms, Japan has several still standing castles that you could visit during your stay, but a good time to visit these ancient structures is during the cherry blossom season

Torii Gates at Fushimi Inari shrine Kyoto, a stunning winding path of Torii gates that lead up to the Main shrine of Fushimi Inari, a stunning photo location as well a great walk


Popular Places to eat

Koba More, is a popular restaurant located near Himeji castle, serving up the best gyoza and ramen for miles around, has a great atmosphere and friendly servers

Kura Sushi, a popular restaurant that has a conveyor belt system that delivers the dishes to the customer, where you can help yourself the the sushi you want

Gyopao Gyoza, is a great location to experience a wide varity of Japanese dishes, a popular and tasty location to visit for a full guide though Japans culinary arts
