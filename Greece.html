WELCOME TO GREECE

Places you should visit

Parthenon in Athens , enjoy the stunning history from the Parthenon located right in the heart of Athens, with stunning views stretching across the metropolitan

Olympia Archaeological Ruins. Delve back into ancient times with exploring the Olympia ruins, along side the original site of the statue of zeus one of the 7 wonders, you can enjoy the site and founding for the olympic games


Santorini is a lush costal resort island located in the aegean sea, you can enjoy exploring the island as well as costal activities. Not to mention the beaches and local food

Rhodes island. Rhodes island site of the famous colossus or Rhodes, has plenty to explore and do on the island for all ages, between warm sunny beaches to historical ruins as well as both on land and sea activities you will be hard pressed to be bored


Popular Places to eat


Koukaki, is a popular street in Athens that offers several storefronts that offer local cuisine, for a reasonable price, with the stunning food and atmosphere your be sure to have your fill here

Many restaurant in Rhodes offer a wide variety of local cuisine some even offer coastal outdoor seating so you can enjoy your food with a gentle coastal breeze

Thira in Santorini is a popular site to visit for it's traditional delicacies and stunning views
